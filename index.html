Students Visa

Students Visa

A student visa allows international students to study abroad legally, requiring admission to a recognized institution, financial proof, and language proficiency. It may also offer part-time work and post-study opportunities, ensuring a smooth academic journey while complying with immigration regulations.

Tourists Visa

Tourist Visa

A tourist visa allows short-term travel for leisure or sightseeing, typically requiring proof of funds, a travel itinerary, and return tickets. It does not permit employment and must comply with immigration regulations, ensuring hassle-free travel.
